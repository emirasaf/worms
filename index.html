WHAT IS WORMS?

Worms is a cryptocurrency based marketplace. You can use your cryptocurrencies for shopping. Worms works with Binance so, you can make payments for your orders quickly!

You can create orders quickly with fast buy feature! With this feature; you can complete directly an order without go shopping cart.

Worms is totally safe. We use Binance's Payment API so, all the transactions are process in your cryptocurrency exchange.

Worms supports +100 cryptocurrencies!

You can easily connect your Binance account and start shopping!
BENEFITS OF WORMS
Fast
You can shop quickly in Worms. User-friendly UI and already securely saved personal informations convides quick shopping experience.
Secure
Worms works with Binance so, your cryptocurrencies and personal informations are keeping securely. Worms can't reach your any personal data. These process are totally automated.
Cashback
You can earn certain amounts of cashback from the purchases you create in Worms. Cashbacks are sometimes given in the cryptocurrency you spend, sometimes in Worms Token. You can send these cashbacks to your cryptocurrency exchange or keep them in Worms Wallet!
